About MediLink
MediLink is a comprehensive, end-to-end platform designed to streamline medical appointment and health record management. Offering seamless experiences for both patients and doctors, MediLink enables easy appointment booking, secure profile management, and efficient digital handling of medical reports (including OCR scanning). Accessible via both a user-friendly web interface and a dedicated mobile application, MediLink is built to simplify healthcare interactions and empower better health management.
